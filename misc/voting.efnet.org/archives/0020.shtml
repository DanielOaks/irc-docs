Chris (comstud) has coded a reverse command for the opless channel module of
services.int, originally Tsk's idea.

In the event that a channel is taken over (via a compromised machine, hacked server,
etc), any global oper can issue a "/msg services reverse #channel" command.  Services
will join a client to the channel, lower ts by 1, and op the top-5-most-opped people
according to its database.

I don't feel the need for more than 1 oper to concur on a reverse command, as more
than one oper may not know the channel's story and requiring such would create a game
of opers convincing each other to issue the command.
