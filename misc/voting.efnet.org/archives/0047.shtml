Description:


This vote is to grant services.eu admins a two week period
to run OpenChanfix in full production. In order for this to
happen, services.int admins will have to put Chanfix in data
collection mode only. This vote by no means approves
OpenChanfix as a replacement of Chanfix. The exact time
frame is to be worked out with the admins of services.int
and services.eu. The two week test period must happen before
the end of 2004. After the two week test period, OpenChanfix
must return back to data collection only mode and Chanfix to
return to full production mode.

This two week test period may be canceled if the admins of
services.eu and services.int collectively agree, due to any
problem with OpenChanfix.
