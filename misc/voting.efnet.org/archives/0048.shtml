Description:


Global gline requirement

This vote will require all servers on EFnet to have glines
enabled. The gline expiration time may be decided by
individual servers, but it can not be less than 6 hours, or
more than 7 days.

If the vote is approved, all servers will be required to
activate glines within a 14 day period. The 14 days will
begin on the day this vote closes. Failure to comply will
result in the server(s) being removed from the network.

If the vote do not pass, it will not affect the european
regional rule on gline requirement.
