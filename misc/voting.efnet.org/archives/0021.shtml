Status: PASSED (yes: 22, no: 0, abstain: 0, elig: 29)

Description:


This vote is on the migration from TS3 to TS5 as 
implemented in the technical document sent to the voting 
list.  Specifically, this vote will remove the TS exeption 
making it effectively impossible to hack ops on a split.

This vote should in no way "tie our hands", as we now have 
services.int.  If realized in the future that we still 
require an additional way to regain ops (to replace opers 
being able to hack ops on splits), we can vote on a means 
to do so in the future.  For now, we need to take this 
preventative measure to reduce attacks on our servers.

Per this vote, all admins will be required to upgrade their 
ircds to one that supports TS5 by the end of September.

TS5 should not be enabled on ANY server until Saturday 
October 6th, at which time admins will have until the end 
of Sunday October 7th to enable the feature in their ircds 
(48 hrs).

The TS5 specification is at:

https://voting.efnet.org/docs/ts5.txt

The implementation may be slightly modified, but the 
effects the same.
