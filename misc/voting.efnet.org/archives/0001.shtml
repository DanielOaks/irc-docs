Status: PASSED (yes: 20, no: 0, abstain: 0, elig: ~25)

Description:


1) Definition of a majority vote

A vote will be considered "passed" if:

(number of yes votes) / (number of cast votes) >= 51%, provided that at least 51%
of eligible servers cast a vote.

2) Duration of vote process

Vote duration will be left up to the caller.  It is suggested that most votes be
given 2 weeks to run their course.  In extreme cases where there is good reason to
call a vote with a shorter duration, the 51% turnout requirement will still hold
in order to reach a majority.

3) Voter eligibility

It is up to each admin who may vote on behalf of their server.  Admins with more
than one server may cast votes or elect persons to cast votes for each server
provided that the servers are considered seperate entities by the admin community
(i.e. irc.west.gblx.net and irc.east.gblx.net receive one vote, irc.total.net and
irc.magic.ca receive two votes).
