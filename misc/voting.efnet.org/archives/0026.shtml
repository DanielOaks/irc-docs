US Voting System

Open to US admins only, this vote is to determine the will
of the US admin body as a whole so we can either move
forward or put this issue to rest.

Do you think the US portion of EFnet should continue with a
routing committee or do you prefer we form a US-wide voting
system?

There has been some discussion and proposals put forth but
participation from the admin body has been very limited
which leads me to believe there isn't much interest in a US-
wide voting system.

If we vote in favor of a US-wide system we should see ALL
admins put forth some suggestions and participate in the
discussions.

Voting YES indicates favor of a US-wide voting system.
Voting NO indicates favor of the Routing Committee.
