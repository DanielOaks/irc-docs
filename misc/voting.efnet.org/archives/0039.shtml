The following vote has closed:
                                                                                
Gline list addition: Flooding
                                                                                
This vote is to add flooding as an acceptable Gline
reason.  Rather than defining a specific number of messages
per minute or something similar, the definition of flooding
will still be left to the discression of the opers placing
the Gline.  However, let it be said that a client Glined
for flooding must truly be disrupting network operations
(again, at the discression of the 3 opers placing the
Gline).
